Why VR is not a fad

Domesticating Reality

  • Initial discovery/Euphoria
  • Love/hate
  • Transparency
  • Euphoria - it's the coolest thing ever! So many possibilities
  • Love/hate - the technology is both the best and worst thing ever
  • Transparency - the technology is adopted into everyday life
  • DocLab: Domesticating Reality; Voice of VR podcast
  • https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Domestication_theory

What prevents adoption?

  • Lack of clear benefit
  • friction
We're currently in love/hate w/ VR.

What irreplaceable benefits does VR offer?

  • VR creates space
  • VR provides presence and agency
* Virtual space is unlimited and cheap to create. (alternative build a building?) * VR provides immediate presence, connecting people over distances (alternative airplane & meet in person)
